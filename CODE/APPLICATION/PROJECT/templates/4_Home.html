SOLAR POWER

GENERATION

Solar power generation harnesses sunlight to produce electricity, using photovoltaic cells or solar panels. These panels convert sunlight into direct current (DC) electricity. An inverter then transforms this into alternating current (AC), suitable for use in homes and businesses. Solar power is a sustainable and renewable energy source, reducing reliance on fossil fuels and lowering carbon emissions.

SOLAR POWER

RADIATIONS

Solar power radiations are electromagnetic waves emitted by the sun, encompassing visible light, infrared, and ultraviolet rays. These radiations drive various renewable energy technologies like photovoltaic cells and solar thermal systems, offering sustainable alternatives to traditional energy sources. Harnessing solar power radiations holds significant potential for reducing carbon emissions and mitigating climate change impacts.
